Company Profile of MN Industries.

M.N. Industries a highly reputed ISO 9001-2008 certified manufacture of mechanical shaft seals for the various pumps.  It caters to the well known OEM’s in the field of Agriculture Pumps, Diesel engines, pumps, food industries and other engineering industries too.

The company commenced its operation in the year 2000 and has earned a high reputation with its customers on the on the strength of its quality and services. MNI today is the most preferred vendor for its customers.

The customers from Agro chemicals, Chemicals, Cement, Dyes and its intermediate, Bulk drugs pharmaceutical, steel industries etc. have expressed their confidence and satisfaction by placing repeated ever growing orders.

MNI has developed its capacities and capabilities to cater to supply very vide range of mechanical shaft seals to the required quality and quantity irrespective of batch sizes and that too at the international standards.

Our management has the a rich experience of more than two decades in this field of shaft seal manufacturing which builds our competitive and value for money supplies.

We as a company have an ever growing ambition to expand our market and approach all kind and size of customers and their requirements to provide the best services.
